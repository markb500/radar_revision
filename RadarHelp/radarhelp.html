Overview

This app is intended for use as a supplemental revision aid in your preparation for the Radar exam. It should be used alongside the practice questions provided in the training notes and in consultation with your Tutor.

Instructions For Use

Choose a button from the left column for the subject you wish to revise. Each click will produce a new question. Once you have completed your answer on paper, click the ‘solution’ button to see a full solution. A counter within the Solution button shows the number of times the solution has been viewed.

Link To Training Notes

Under each question, a link is provided which will take you to the relevant area of the training notes.

Test Designer

Allows you to create a test in a design of your choosing, which can be printed or presented as a pdf. Instructions are given on each page.
